Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is to help people learn more about the saxophone. The site will explain what a saxophone is, its history, and the different types that exist. It will also share why I love this instrument and how it has influenced music around the world.

Audience

The website is designed for anyone who is interested in learning about the saxophone, especially beginners, music students, and people who enjoy musical instruments. Visitors may not have much background knowledge about the saxophone, so the content will be easy to understand and informative.
